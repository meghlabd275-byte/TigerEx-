Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start trading?+
Register an account, complete verification, deposit funds, and navigate to Trade to start buying and selling cryptocurrencies.
What are the trading fees?+
Maker fee: 0.1%, Taker fee: 0.1%. VIP users get discounted rates starting from 0.08%.
How do I enable 2FA?+
Go to Profile → Security → Enable 2FA. Scan the QR code with Google Authenticator or Authy app.
What is the minimum deposit?+
No minimum for crypto deposits. For fiat, the minimum is $10 via bank transfer.
How long do withdrawals take?+
Crypto withdrawals are processed within 5 minutes. Fiat withdrawals take 1-3 business days.
