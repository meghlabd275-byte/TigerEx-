### 1. Options Trading
- **Options for BTC, ETH, SOL**: 0.02% fees
- **Multiple expiration dates**: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly
- **High liquidity**: $11.3B 30-day volume, $2.16B open interest
- **Advanced strategies**: Selling puts, buying puts, selling naked calls

### 2. Trading Bots (5 Categories)
- **Futures Grid**: Grid trading with leverage
- **Futures Martingale**: Doubling down on losses for reversal profits
- **Futures Combo**: Portfolio rebalancing of perpetual contracts
- **Spot Grid**: Spot market grid trading
- **DCA**: Dollar cost averaging strategy
- **Aurora AI**: AI-powered bot recommendations
- **Bot Copy Trading**: Copy successful trading bots

### 3. Copy Trading
- **Real-time trade mirroring** from expert traders
- **Filter metrics**: ROI, PnL, win rate, stability index, drawdown, followers
- **Time frames**: 7, 30, 90 day analysis
- **Proportional or fixed margin** trading

- **Bybit Earn**: 8 high-yield savings products

  - Fund Pool: 0.60%-10.00% BTC, 4.50%-20.00% USDT
  - Dual Asset: Up to 901.35% APY
  - Liquidity Mining: Up to 1066% APY
- **Crypto Loans**: Collateralized borrowing for trading

### 5. MetaTrader 5 Integration
- **24/7/365 trading** for gold, FOREX, commodities
- **Up to 500X leverage**
- **100+ trading pairs**
- **Seamless fund transfers** between Bybit and MetaTrader
